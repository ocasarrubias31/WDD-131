Reflecting on past traumas

Past traumas are hard to pinpint. As we grow into adulthood we may find less time to deal with our problems and it seems easier to set aside.

According to, How Trauma May Impact Adults, It says “Trauma can lead to PTSD”. This is why it's important get unresolved trauma solved According to the article in Help.org “Trauma affects more than just your mind. It can also have long-term physical consequences. “ The article explains how your body keeps stress hormones high which can alter blood pressure. What we can take from this explanation is that when stress hormones are present at all times resulting from trauma an adult cannot live and maintain a healthy life. Focus will be lost, constant questioning of oneself can lead to lost opportunities and a closure to people around them.

Why can past traumas effect future?

Past traumas can effect future due to the inablility to handle certain situations, that you would be able to handle easier without the trauams. Opertunites may be missed becuase past traumas cause self esteem issues, anger managment issues and issues missing out on oppurtinites and losing connections. This can be due to the inablility to over come them. Once identified, we are able to hit the source and work on healing from them.
