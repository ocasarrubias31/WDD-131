FAQs

Is HCT a wallet, an exchange, or both?

HCT is a unified platform: institutional custody with integrated execution and reporting. You keep control through policy-based permissions.

Where are client assets held?

Assets are stored in segregated addresses with tiered cold/warm storage. Fiat is held at partner banks in client-segregated accounts.

Do you support APIs?

Yes—REST and WebSocket for market data, orders, balances, and reporting. Sandbox available.
